Microtonal Explorer

A tool for exploring and creating microtonal music

Project Overview

The Microtonal Explorer is a specialized application designed to help musicians and composers explore the vast world of microtonal music. This tool provides an intuitive interface for working with alternative tuning systems beyond the standard 12-tone equal temperament.

Key Features

Custom Scale Creation

Design and save your own microtonal scales with precise frequency ratios and intervals.

Real-time Synthesis

Hear your microtonal creations instantly with built-in sound synthesis capabilities.

Scale Analysis

Analyze the mathematical relationships between intervals in your custom scales.
